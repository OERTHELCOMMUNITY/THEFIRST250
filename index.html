Frequently asked questions

Everything about OERTHEL
espresso in a can

What is OERTHEL?

OERTHEL is the first pure espresso in a can from the Netherlands. Made from 100% Arabica cold brew, cold brewed for 18 hours. 200mg caffeine per can. Zero sugar. Zero sweeteners. Meant as an adult alternative to energy drinks.

How much caffeine is in a can of OERTHEL?

Every can of OERTHEL contains 200mg of caffeine, from 100% Arabica cold brew espresso. That's comparable to two to three regular espressos, but in can form — without sugar and without synthetic additives.

What's the difference between OERTHEL and an energy drink?

Energy drinks contain on average 27g of sugar, artificial flavors and synthetic caffeine. OERTHEL contains only cold brew espresso from 100% Arabica beans. No sugar. No sweeteners. No synthetic stimulants. Pure natural caffeine — for people who want focus, not a sugar spike.

Is OERTHEL sugar-free?

Yes. Both variants — OERTHEL BLACK and OERTHEL WHITE — contain zero sugar and zero sweeteners. No stevia, no erythritol, no aspartame. Just coffee.

What is cold brew espresso?

Cold brew espresso is cold-extracted at low temperature for 18 hours. This gives a richer, less acidic taste than regular espresso, while retaining all the natural caffeine. OERTHEL uses Indonesian Arabica beans, medium dark roast, for a deep chocolate and nutty flavor.

Who is OERTHEL for?

OERTHEL is for adults who consciously choose functional coffee over sugar-laden energy drinks. Professionals, athletes, creatives — anyone who wants focus and energy without the sugar spike and crash of regular energy drinks.
